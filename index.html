China's Burgeoning Space-based Spy Program

  • view the story

China's spy satellite program

has been rapidly expanding in recent years; rivaling that of the United States. In 2014 alone, China launched seven of what western military experts believe are spy satellites. There are still more than twice as many US spy satellites, but half of those were launched before 2009. More than half of China's active spy satellites were launched between 2011 and 2014.
